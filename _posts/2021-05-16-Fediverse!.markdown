What is fediverse? Well it's the best thing you can ever imagine if you are a social media person, and is already tired of most social media like Facebook, Instagram and twitter. Basically fediverse is the other beautiful side of the internet. It's big but it's not a single body it's a huge collection of thousands of servers hosted all around the globe by not multi million or multi billion dollar companies but by normal people like you and me. 


Fediverse is a huge network made up of many platforms like mastodon, pleroma, peertube, GNU social, pixelFed and so much more. And each and every one of those are hosted by thousands of people. If you have the resources and technology, even you can host those. Well out of those above mentioned platform the most famous one is mastodon, and it's like a twitter clone but just many times better. Not just because it's decentralized but also there are a shit ton of more features. 


And how this work is, just imagine that you created a mastodon account. It's just a gateway to access the fediverse, you get not only mastodon content but also content from other servers platforms like pleroma and GNU social. Everything is connected. You can even interact with people from other platforms and their toots(tweets version in mastodon) with no problem. Isn't it just amazing? It's like interacting with twitter using your facebook account! Isn't it wonderful? Well even if it was possible, fediverse is much more better because there's no central ownership. Do you remember that recent case where Twitter deleted Donald Trump's account just because having an opinion? (I don't like him but ...) Well oh and even to do so, twitter took some decent amount of time. That will not happen in the Fediverse. I mean if you host your own server then you are in charge of all your content and no one wil be able to remove you. And in the other hand if some one posted something unethical on your server then you will have to wait like twitter had to. And just remove the person in question. But don't worry in most cases everything is handled nicely in fediverse. And here on fediverse no one will sell your personal information to advertisement companies like Facebook or Instagram.
