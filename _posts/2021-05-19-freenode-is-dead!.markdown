Hello there folks, welcome back with a new post. But sadly this ain't a good news. This is more like a new announcement than an actual post. I just wanted to make sure that you guys are aware of the present state of freenode. What happened was sometime ago Christel, the former head of the freenode staff has sold 'freenode ltd' to a third party, Andrew Lee. Under terms that are not disclosed even to the staff. And the thing is even though Mr Lee at that time promised to never exercise any operational control over freenode. 
And apparently it wasn't that true, considering some actions of Mr Lee in the past few weeks. It seems to be soon Mr Lee will be legally taking over the entire freenode network, including the user data. The freenode staff and some like minded people tried to fight off this take over, but legally it wasn't easy. So Mr Lee taking over freenode is unstoppable.


This means if you had a freenode account, your personal data could be at risk. We can't be sure that Mr Lee won't be selling those information. So most of the freenode staff will be resign freenode to create a new better network for the like minded people. It will be called libera.chat and you can connect to the network at `irc.libera.chat`, ssl port 6697 (and the usual clearnet port). 


And just to be sure, freenode staffer Kline recommends replacing your email and password with other values.
